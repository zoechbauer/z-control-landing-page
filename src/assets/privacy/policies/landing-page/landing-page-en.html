1. Introduction

This privacy notice explains what data is processed by the z-control Landing Page App, why it is processed, and what rights you have. The landing page does not collect personal data unless you actively provide it (for example via a contact link).

2. Collected Data & Purpose

  • Anonymous usage statistics: We collect anonymous, non‑identifying usage data (e.g. page views, opened sections, button clicks) solely to improve the user experience and for error analysis.
  • Purpose: Product improvement, content optimization and debugging.

3. Legal Basis

Analytics processing is performed only after your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You may withdraw consent at any time. No analytics data will be sent to third parties without your consent.

4. Data Recipients

We use Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) for analytics. Anonymous usage data is transmitted to and processed by Google/Firebase. Google may store data on servers located in various countries (including the USA). See Google’s privacy policy for details.

5. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained according to Firebase/Google retention settings. We do not combine anonymous analytics data with personally identifiable information.

6. Withdrawal & Opt‑out

You can withdraw consent at any time through the app’s settings. Alternatively, clear local settings in your browser (e.g. localStorage) or disable analytics via the provided option. After withdrawal, no further analytics events will be sent.

Note (developer/test): To disable collection locally, run in the browser console: localStorage.setItem('analytics_consent','false'); location.reload();

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of or port your data. To exercise your rights or for privacy inquiries contact the address below.

8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect data. Absolute security cannot be guaranteed, but we follow established practices and reputable service providers.

9. Children’s Privacy

The site is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Major changes will be communicated via the app or website.

11. Contact
