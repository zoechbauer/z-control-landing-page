🔒 Data Privacy & Protection

This privacy policy explains how the z-control QR Code Generator App collects, uses, and protects your information.

📱 Data Collection

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect personal information unless explicitly provided by you.
  • Email Addresses: Only stored locally on your device for QR code delivery convenience.
  • QR Code Content: Text you enter is processed locally and not transmitted to our servers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking technologies.

💾 Local Data Storage

  • Device Storage: All data (email addresses, preferences) stored locally on your device.
  • Automatic Cleanup: In the Mobile App, all generated QR code files older than 3 hours are automatically deleted upon app restart.
  • User Control: You can delete all stored data anytime through app settings.

🌐 Web App vs Mobile App

  • Web App: Uses the browser's local storage; no server communication takes place. For privacy reasons, generated QR code files are not deleted automatically.
  • Mobile App: Uses the device's IndexedDB storage. Generated QR code files are automatically deleted.
  • No Cloud Sync: Data is never synchronized across devices.

📧 Email Functionality

  • Local Processing: QR codes generated locally on your device.
  • Email Integration: Uses your device's email app - we don't handle email transmission.
  • No Email Monitoring: We cannot see or access your emails.

🔓 Open Source Transparency

This app is open source. You can review our code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or our website.
